How to Find your Yahoo Email Account?

Simple steps to locate and recover your Yahoo Mail account

Check Your Saved Login Information

If you have previously signed in to your Yahoo email account on a device or browser, your email address may be saved in the browser settings or password manager. Check your saved credentials in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.

Search Your Emails from Other Accounts

Look through emails from other email accounts you use. Yahoo often sends verification messages, security alerts, or welcome emails that contain your Yahoo email address.

Use Yahoo Account Recovery

Visit the Yahoo sign-in helper page and choose the option to recover your account. You can enter your recovery phone number or alternate email address to find your Yahoo email account.

Check Devices Where You Signed In Before

If you have used Yahoo Mail on a smartphone, tablet, or desktop email app, open the mail settings on that device to view the configured Yahoo email address.

Contact Yahoo Support

If none of the above methods work, Yahoo Support can guide you through the verification process to help locate your Yahoo email account securely.
