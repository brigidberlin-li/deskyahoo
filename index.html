How to Get Started with Yahoo! Account?

Follow 3 simple steps and Access your Yahoo! email Account?

Already Have an Yahoo! Account!

If you don't have an account, first create one.

Connect with Live Chat!

Join Live Chat. Representatives are available 24/7 to help you.

Go to your Yahoo! Dashboard

Follow the guidelines provided by the representative and get connected with your account dashboard.

Recover your Yahoo Account

  • Go to the Yahoo Sign-In Helper page in your web browser.
  • Enter your Yahoo email address or recovery phone number and tap Continue.
  • Choose a recovery option (SMS or recovery email) to receive a verification code.
  • Enter the verification code to confirm your identity.
  • Create a new password save it, and sign in to your Yahoo account.
